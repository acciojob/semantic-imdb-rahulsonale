Interstellar Summary

Interstellar is a science-fiction epic directed by Christopher Nolan. Set in a near future where Earth is dying, the film follows a group of astronauts traveling through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity. It beautifully blends science, emotion, and breathtaking visuals to explore love, time, and survival.

Actors

  • Matthew McConaughey as Cooper
  • Anne Hathaway as Amelia Brand
  • Jessica Chastain as Murph
  • Michael Caine as Professor Brand
